# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Top Findings

1. **Unprecedented Growth in Early 2025:** Revenue and new customer registrations saw massive, synchronized spikes in Q1/Q2 2025, with monthly revenue doubling its baseline to peak at over 5,000 JOD in June 2025.
2. **Books are the Primary Revenue Driver:** Defying expectations, the "Books" category out-performed high-ticket items like Electronics, generating the highest total revenue at approximately 11,000 JOD.
3. **Location Dictates Purchasing Power:** Customers residing in Madaba and Amman generate the highest Average Order Value (AOV), proving that geographical location strongly influences customer spending habits.

## Supporting Data

* **For Finding 1:** Supported by the Monthly Revenue Trend and Monthly Customer Registrations KPIs. The synchronized spikes in March and June are visually confirmed in `kpi1_monthly_revenue.png` and `kpi5_customer_growth.png`.
* **For Finding 2:** Supported by the Revenue by Category KPI and validated by a One-way ANOVA test, which confirmed a statistically significant difference in revenue generation across different product groups. See `kpi4_revenue_by_category.png`.
* **For Finding 3:** Supported by the Average Order Value by City KPI and validated by an Independent T-Test (Amman vs. Irbid), confirming the variance in spending is statistically significant and not due to chance. See `kpi3_aov_by_city.png`.

## Recommendations

1. **Expand and Promote the Books Category:** Since books are the proven top revenue generator, the purchasing team should expand the literary catalog, and marketing should run targeted book-bundle campaigns (e.g., "Buy 2 Get 1 Free") on the homepage.
2. **Replicate the March/June 2025 Marketing Strategy:** The massive influx of revenue and new users during these months indicates a highly successful campaign or seasonal trend. The team must audit what drove this traffic and heavily fund similar initiatives in upcoming quarters.
3. **Target High-Value Cities with Premium Ads:** Given the significantly higher Average Order Values in Madaba and Amman, digital ad spend should be reallocated to target these specific regions with high-margin, premium products to maximize Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).
