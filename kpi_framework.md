# KPI Framework — Amman Digital Market

Define 5 KPIs for the Amman Digital Market. At least 2 must be time-based and 1 must be cohort-based.

---

## KPI 1

- **Name:** Monthly Revenue Trend (Time-based)
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated per month to track overall business growth.
- **Formula:** `SUM(quantity * unit_price)` grouped by `order_month`
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** `orders`, `order_items`, `products`
- **Baseline Value:** ~2,500 JOD average, peaking at >5,000 JOD in June 2025.
- **Interpretation:** Shows seasonal spikes and overall growth trajectory. Massive spikes in March and June indicate highly successful marketing campaigns or seasonal demand that should be investigated and replicated.

---

## KPI 2

- **Name:** Weekly Order Volume (Time-based)
- **Definition:** Number of unique orders placed each week.
- **Formula:** `COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)` grouped by `order_week`
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** `orders`
- **Baseline Value:** Stable at ~6 orders/week, with sudden spikes up to 21 orders.
- **Interpretation:** Helps operations and logistics teams anticipate periods of high volume to ensure adequate staffing, shipping capacity, and inventory readiness.

---

## KPI 3

- **Name:** Average Order Value (AOV) by City (Cohort-based)
- **Definition:** The average amount spent per order, segmented by the customer's city.
- **Formula:** `AVG(order_revenue)` grouped by `city`
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** `orders`, `order_items`, `products`, `customers`
- **Baseline Value:** Madaba and Amman lead with the highest median order values.
- **Interpretation:** Indicates geographical purchasing power. This helps the marketing team target premium products (like Electronics) to high-value cities to maximize return on ad spend (ROAS).

---

## KPI 4

- **Name:** Total Revenue by Product Category (Cohort-based)
- **Definition:** Aggregate revenue generated by each product category.
- **Formula:** `SUM(quantity * unit_price)` grouped by `category`
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** `order_items`, `products`
- **Baseline Value:** Books lead at ~11,000 JOD, followed closely by Electronics.
- **Interpretation:** Highlights the most lucrative product lines. Knowing that Books out-perform Electronics allows the purchasing team to focus on expanding the literary catalog and running book-specific promotions.

---

## KPI 5

- **Name:** Monthly Customer Registrations (Time-based)
- **Definition:** Number of new users signing up per month.
- **Formula:** `COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id)` grouped by `registration_month`
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** `customers`
- **Baseline Value:** Averages 3-4 users/month, with a huge spike to 9 users in March 2025.
- **Interpretation:** Measures the effectiveness of top-of-funnel marketing efforts and brand awareness. The March 2025 spike correlates directly with the revenue spike, proving that user acquisition directly drove that month's success.
